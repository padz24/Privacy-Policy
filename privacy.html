🛡️ Privacy Policy for AnonyBotz

Last updated: August 3, 2025

📌 Information We Collect

  • Telegram User IDs and usernames of those interacting with the bot.
  • Group chat metadata such as group ID, title, and roles (e.g., admin status).
  • Messages sent to the bot (commands or inline queries), temporarily and for processing only.

🔐 How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used to:

  • Verify users and group administrators.
  • Respond to bot commands or automate moderation.
  • Track group activity or user status via Supabase for moderation/history logs.

🧾 Data Storage

We store data as follows:

  • In a secured Supabase database.
  • Bot credentials are stored safely in .env files and never shared.
  • No data is shared or sold to third parties.

⏳ Data Retention

Messages are processed in real-time and only stored when necessary. Users can request deletion of their data by contacting the bot administrator.

🔒 Security

  • Use of environment variables for sensitive information.
  • Admin verification and access control using Telegram's role metadata.
  • Authenticated access to database operations.

👤 User Rights

You may request:

  • Information about what data has been stored.
  • Deletion of your data from our servers.
  • To remove the bot from your groups at any time.

📞 Contact

For questions or data-related requests, contact the bot owner via Telegram.
